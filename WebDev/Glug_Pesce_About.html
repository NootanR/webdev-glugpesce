How It All Started Up!

GLUG-PESCE was started few years back by the alumnis Rakesh, Manu and many others of Information science & Engineering and Computer science & Engineering department with the vision of spreading knowledge about various free and open softwares and the use of linux operating systems instead of the traditional windows operating system. Later on it was carried by seniors Akshay Gudi etc.

This year, FSMK (Free Software Movement Karnataka) organization had organized a technical summer camp from 20th to 26th of July. FSMK is a non-profit organization working for spreading free software and its ideals. The camp was very informative and grasping. Four students, Ramyashree, Niveditha, Shrunga and Archana from our college had participated in it. It was this event that triggered the interest of the four and motivated them to come up with new ideas for GLUG-PESCE. They approached the resource persons of FSMK in the camp and discussed certain views with them. They were assured with complete support.

As our academic year started, the four of them contacted few of their fellow mates who were also interested in GLUG and presented the new ideas before them. Thus the team expanded to nine members. They all had several meetings regarding this and finally came up with an organized plan to implement the ideas in the GLUG. The core team approached the respected HODs of Computer Science Department and Information Science Department and requested them for their mentoring for which they gladly consented. Henceforth GLUG-PESCE 2015 has taken a new vibrant form and is been organizing various events.
